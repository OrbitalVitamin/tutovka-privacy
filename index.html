Privacy Policy for Tutovka

Last updated: April 2026

Tutovka is a football quiz app. This policy explains what data we collect and why.

Data We Collect

  • Anonymous user ID (Firebase) — to link your scores to the leaderboard
  • Display name — only if you choose to appear on the leaderboard
  • Game scores — stored on our leaderboard
  • Push notification token — to send you notifications (requires your permission)
  • Advertising data via Google AdMob — to show relevant ads (requires your permission on iOS)

Third-Party Services

  • Google AdMob: may collect device identifiers for advertising. Privacy policy
  • Firebase (Google): used for authentication, database, and notifications. Privacy policy

Data Retention

Data is retained while you use the app. To request deletion, contact us at the email below.

Children

This app is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.
